# ThisSongThatTime Story Creation Prompts - Complete Reference

## Basic Information
- **Story Title**: "Give your story a memorable headline..."
- **Author Name**: "What should we call you?" (Real name, stage name, or secret identity)

## Core Story Details (Required)

### Age & Life Context
- **Age**: "How old were you? (approximate is fine)"
  - Options: 0-9, 10-14, 15-19, 20s, 30s, 40s, 50+
- **Life Context**: "What was happening in your life at the time?"
  - Placeholder: "e.g., Starting college, going through a breakup..."

### Key Story Elements
- **Discovery Moment**: "When and how did you first encounter this song?"
  - Placeholder: "Describe how you discovered this song..."
- **Core Memory**: "Describe the most significant memory connected to this song"
  - Placeholder: "Tell us about the moment this song became meaningful (2-3 sentences minimum)..."
  - Note: Minimum 2-3 sentences required
- **Emotional Connection**: "What feelings does this song evoke for you now?"
  - Placeholder: "Describe the emotions this song brings up..."

## Optional Deep Dive Prompts
*"These questions often reveal details and emotions you didn't know you remembered. Choose any that spark something for you."*

### ✧ Sensory & Setting
- **The Scene**: "Paint the picture - what did you see, smell, feel?"
- **Soundtrack Moment**: "A specific moment when this song was playing that changed everything?"
- **Seasonal Connection**: "Do you associate this song with a particular season or weather?"

### ♪ Relationships & People
- **Shared Experience**: "Who else was part of this song's story?"
- **Musical Introduction**: "Did someone special introduce you to this song?"
- **Generational Bridge**: "Does this song connect you to family, friends, or community?"

### ✦ Personal Growth & Change
- **Before & After**: "How were you different before and after this song became important?"
- **Life Transition**: "Was this song the soundtrack to a major change or milestone?"
- **Comfort & Healing**: "Has this song been a source of comfort during difficult times?"
- **Identity Marker**: "Does this song represent who you are or were becoming?"

### ♫ Musical Elements
- **The Hook**: "What specific part captures you most?"
- **Lyrical Resonance**: "Particular lyrics that speak to you?"
- **Musical Discovery**: "Did this song introduce you to a new genre, artist, or era?"

### ◈ Broader Context
- **Cultural Moment**: "Connect to a larger cultural moment you lived through?"
- **Unexpected Connection**: "Something surprising about your relationship with this song?"
- **Legacy & Impact**: "How has this song influenced your taste or life path?"
- **Sharing & Passing On**: "Have you shared this song with others?"

### ✨ Reflection Prompts
- **Message to Past Self**: "What would you tell the person you were when this song first mattered?"
- **Song as Compass**: "What does this song teach you about what you value?"
- **Future Connection**: "Will this song still be meaningful in 10 years?"

## Publishing Options

### Format Selection
Users choose from three publishing formats:
1. **Q&A Format**: "Your responses organized as questions and answers"
2. **Free Writing**: "Your story told in your own flowing narrative"
3. **AI-Assisted Writing**: "Polished story crafted from your responses"

### AI Tone Options (for AI-Assisted Writing only)
- **Warm & Personal**: "Intimate and heartfelt, like sharing with a close friend"
- **Reflective & Thoughtful**: "Contemplative and introspective, exploring deeper meanings"
- **Nostalgic & Poetic**: "Lyrical and evocative, capturing the beauty of memory"
- **Conversational & Relatable**: "Easy-going and accessible, connecting with everyone"

### Publication Options
- **Save as Draft**: Save privately to continue later
- **Publish Story**: Make public immediately on the platform
